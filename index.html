Frequently Asked Questions

How long does an audit take?

Most audits complete within 3-5 minutes, depending on the size and complexity of your website.

What changes are made to my website?

We create an optimized version internally for comparison. No changes are made to your live website without your explicit approval.

Can I audit password-protected sites?

Currently, we only support publicly accessible websites. Private sites require special configuration.
